Air Travel

Logan Airport

Boston's Logan Airport services all major domestic and international airlines and most discount airlines including Jet Blue and Southwest

(N.B. The Omni and most other hotels can be reached easily from the airport by the subway or cab. We do not recommend you rent a car.)

Driving

Parking

Parking at the Omni will cost you $25 a day. If you stay at a different hotel, your price may vary. The cheapest option is parking at the Boston Common garage which is $11 per weekend day. It is about a 3 minute walk to the Omni from this garage through the Boston Common. You can drop your luggage off at the hotel and then drop your car off and leave it at the Boston Common Garage for the weekend.

(N.B. We do not recommend you try to find on-street parking. You WILL get a ticket and possibly get your car towed.)

Bus

Boston's Bus station is located at South Station, which is connected to the subway. The station serves all major carriers. It also serves discount carriers that are found in New York City, Philadelphia and Washington D.C.
